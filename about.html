About Us

Who We Are

Builtin Homes is a professional construction company based in Kovilambakkam, South Chennai. We specialize in residential and commercial construction, interior design, and complete turnkey project delivery.

Founded with a simple mission — to make quality construction accessible, transparent, and stress-free — we have grown into a trusted name across South Chennai. Our team comprises experienced engineers, architects, and project managers who are passionate about building spaces that people love.

From the rapidly developing neighbourhoods of Nanmangalam and Kovilambakkam to established areas across South Chennai, Builtin Homes has left its mark on over 100 homes and commercial structures.
